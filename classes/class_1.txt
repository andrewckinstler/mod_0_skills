Class: Refrigerator

Attributes:
temperature (float)
amount_of_food (integer)
available_space (float)
clean (boolean)

Methods:
leave_door_open (subtracts from temperature)
cooking_steak (subtracts 1 from amount_of_food, increases available_space by 1)
dinner_service (changes clean to false)
morning_prep (increases amount_of_food by 30, subtracts 30 from available_space)
